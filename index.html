Our Story

The hardest thing is to share what we feel for each other... with others, while also doing the magic that brought us together justice. We met in June for the first time because it seemed we shared the same interests and fondness for the same things. During the first weeks we were amazed with each others character and that helped us overcome moments that are hurdles for other relationships. When is the right time to say I Love You? When do you meet the parents without having it be weird? All that didn't matter to us. Each step was a natural one and the first I Love You (that Denisa said by the way) just made us feel very happy because we both felt the same way. Weeks turned into months and months into years. We saw that we brought out the best in each other and more. We started traveling, making friends and planning a life. Now we invite you to be a part of the moment where we officialy say 'I do' and promise each other to keep being awesome for years to come. See you there!

FAQ

How to get there?
Well, there are several options, either taxis or grouping up with other out of town people that are attending. If you are having difficulty or need help don't hesitate to contact us!

Where to browse for a hotel?
We actually recommend that you use an AirBnB service instead of paying for a hotel. It's more affordable, closer to the fun and you can share it with another friend!

What should I wear?
We recommend a classy look, which means men have a suit with assorted tie/ribbon and women high heels with assorted dress/suit pants and top. We are going for a more formal wear but make sure you are comfortable!

What if I have a gift for you guys?
We appreciate the fact that you want to help us start our road in life but it's most definitely not mandatory. Our greatest joy is to see you there. If however this is your wish, we will have a special made stand where you can drop your gift off.

So, what are we doing?
Glad you asked! At 12 the ceremony starts and we have to be there for the actual signing and ring offering. After that we will be having 2 hours photo session and at about 2 we will go to the afterparty venue.

Special food/allergies:
If you are vegetarian/vegan or if you have any type of allergies, when you confirm your presence please let us know so that we can have our caterers prepare something special for you so you don't miss out on the fun!
